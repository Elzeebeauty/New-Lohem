OUR OBJECTIVES

  • Home
  • About us

The Main Objectives of LOHEM Shall be to:

1. Create awareness of evangelism not only in towns but also in villages.

2. Serve as a forum for bringing together in unity of faith and strength all members of the Mission.

3. Serve as a forum where people can have fellowship and work in love.

4. Preach the Gospel with the aim of winning souls for Christ.

5. Hold counseling sessions with converts in the office and outreach venues.

6. Follow-up and disciple the converts immediately their lives are given to Christ.

7. Take the Gospel to villages, especially the unreached, win souls, counsel and disciple them.

8. Organize outreaches in Tertiary Institutions, Secondary and Primary Schools with an intention of winning souls for Christ and eliminating cultism.

9. Organize prison and market square outreaches

10. Preach in Hospitals, Rehabilitation centers, Handicapped homes, Reprimand homes, bereaved homes with the intention of restoring the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness of the depressed and distressed as well as introducing them to Christ and discipling them.

11. Facilitate the teaching of the Word by

A. Showing Christian films and

B. Organizing Christian drama

12. Preach God as Jehova Rophl/Rapha and healing of the sick by the power of the Holy Spirit.

13. Preach liberty to the captives, demon possessed and oppressed.

14. Deliver the captives, demon possessed and oppressed.

15. Preach the importance of righteousness

16. Show exemplary, righteous behavior to one another as we initiate our master Christ.

17. Distribute tracts, flyers, Christian literature and Bible when available.

18. Show love to the less privileged through giving of money, household property, materials, clothing, food , drugs etc, especially in unreached villages, Prisons, Rehabilitation centres, Reprimand homes, Handicapped homes, motherless babies homes etc

19. Organize workshops, Seminars and conferences in the office and elsewhere for teaching the Word.

20. Organize the training of Christians on”Leadership in Evangelism and Mission.”

21. Work with some Churches as partners in Evangelism and Mission.

22. Hand over the disciples converts to any living Church as the Mission has no plans of owning any Church.

23. Teach the importance of praying and fasting.

24. Organize praying and fasting Sessions for leaders in Evangelism.

25. Encourage Churches for the support of mission work.

26. Support Missionaries in the field through prayers and financial aid.

27. Motivate and mobilize lukewarm Churches for Evangelism and Mission.

28. Create the forum for holding inter-denominational Workshops/Seminars/Conferences for Pastors and Church Workers.

29. Present an opportunity for members to use their talents in serving God in evangelism.

30. Be concerned about the physical conditions of the people and as much as possible, attend to their physical needs while giving them the Spiritual food.
